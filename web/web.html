Dhaka

Dhaka is the capital city of Bangladesh. It is the most populous city in the Bangladesh, with a metropolitan area of over 13 million inhabitants.

Standing on the River Thames, Dhaka has been a major settlement for two millennia, its history going back to its founding by the Romans, who named it Londinium.
